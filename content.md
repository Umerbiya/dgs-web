Our Story
Founded on a legacy of expertise, DAN GTS began in 2015 under the visionary leadership of Ato Daniel Gebretatiyos Solomon. With over 25 years of deep-rooted experience in business, finance, and the logistics sector, Ato Daniel established this privately-owned enterprise to be a dynamic force in Ethiopian trade. What started as a dedicated sole proprietorship has rapidly evolved into a premier partner for industry, driven by integrity and a commitment to national progress.
about us – experience with a vision for growth
Our Growth Journey: From Investment to Impact
Our trajectory speaks to our reliability and strategic vision. From an initial investment of 20 million Birr, DAN GTS has achieved a milestone turnover exceeding 12 million Birr (as of end 2014 Ethiopian Calendar), demonstrating sustained and remarkable growth. For over a decade, our export operations have also contributed significantly to generating vital foreign currency for Ethiopia’s economy.
Our Comprehensive Portfolio
DAN GTS is your single-source solution across key sectors, specializing in wholesale, trade, and tailored services:
▪
Infrastructure Development: Supplying critical petrochemical products, including asphalt paving grade bitumen and cutbacks, for road construction across Ethiopia and the Horn of Africa.
▪
Industrial Supply: Providing essential steel billets to fuel the domestic steel manufacturing industry.
▪
Nationwide Logistics: Offering reliable road haulage and comprehensive transportation services to move goods efficiently throughout Ethiopia.
▪
Construction Support: Equipping major projects with heavy plant and machinery rental services.
▪
Agricultural Export: Exporting high-quality cereals and oil seeds sourced from regions across Ethiopia.
▪
Sustainable Mobility: Importing heavy-duty trucks and automobiles, with a pioneering focus on retailing electric vehicles in Addis Ababa.
